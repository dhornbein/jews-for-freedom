# About Us

Jews for Freedom is a new collaborative effort bringing Jews together to secure our democracy. Led by partner organizations and energized by many more Jewish social justice groups, we energize and amplify collective actions to bring more Jews into the fight for democracy and against authoritarian power grabs.
